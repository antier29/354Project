ECMAScript


Overview

ECMAScript or European Computer Manufacturers Association Script, is a scripting-language standarization. It was made to help standardize JavaScript. It is most used for client-side scripting and for writing server applications. It was first created in 1997 and the newest version was created in June of 2019.
